About Baymax

Baymax is a fictional superhero character appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics. The idea for Baymax first started with Tadashi's own desire to help everyone in need.


Baymax's characteristics

  1. He's selfless,and caring.
  2. He's naive but curious.
  3. He's very calm, even in dangerous situations.
  4. He always prioritizes the well-being of anyone in distress.
